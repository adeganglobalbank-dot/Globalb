## IMPORTANT NOTICES

1. **Banking License Application:** This certificate of incorporation does not authorize the company to conduct banking operations. A separate banking license from the Central Bank of Nigeria is required.

2. **Minimum Capital Requirements:** The company must maintain the minimum capital requirements as prescribed by the Central Bank of Nigeria for the category of banking license applied for.

3. **Regulatory Compliance:** The company shall comply with all regulatory requirements of the Central Bank of Nigeria, Nigeria Deposit Insurance Corporation, and other relevant regulatory authorities.

4. **Anti-Money Laundering:** The company shall implement robust Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Counter-Terrorism Financing (CTF) measures in accordance with Nigerian laws and international standards.

5. **Data Protection:** The company shall comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and other data protection laws.
