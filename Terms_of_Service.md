**"Account"** means any account maintained by the Customer with the Bank, including savings, current, fixed deposit, and other types of accounts.

**"Bank"** means Global Bank Nigeria Limited, a limited liability company incorporated under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

**"Customer"** means any individual, corporate entity, or organization that maintains an account or uses any service provided by the Bank.

**"CBN"** means the Central Bank of Nigeria.

**"Electronic Banking Services"** means banking services delivered through electronic channels including internet banking, mobile banking, USSD banking, ATM services, and other digital platforms.

**"Funds"** means any money, currency, securities, or other financial instruments held by the Bank on behalf of the Customer.

**"PIN"** means Personal Identification Number used by the Customer to authenticate electronic transactions.

**"Services"** means all banking and financial services provided by the Bank to the Customer.

**"Terms and Conditions"** means these Terms and Conditions of Service and all amendments and modifications thereto.

### 10.1 Bank Liability

**Limited Liability:**
The Bank shall not be liable for:
- Losses resulting from unauthorized transactions where the Customer has failed to maintain security of authentication information
- Losses due to system unavailability or technical issues
- Losses resulting from Customer's negligence or misconduct
- Losses due to force majeure events
- Losses resulting from third-party actions beyond the Bank's control

**Specific Exclusions:**
The Bank shall not be liable for any loss, damage, or expense arising from:
- Unauthorized access to Customer accounts due to Customer's failure to protect authentication information
- Transactions carried out with valid authentication information
- Delays or failures in transaction processing due to third-party service providers
- Incorrect payment instructions provided by the Customer

### 10.2 Customer Liability

**Customer Responsibility:**
The Customer shall be liable for:
- All transactions authorized using valid authentication information
- Losses resulting from failure to report lost or stolen cards or compromised credentials
- Losses resulting from negligence in protecting authentication information
- Losses resulting from providing authentication information to third parties

**Unauthorized Transactions:**
The Customer shall report unauthorized transactions within 48 hours of discovery. Failure to report within this timeframe may result in full liability for the unauthorized transactions.

### 10.3 Indemnity

The Customer shall indemnify the Bank against all losses, damages, claims, expenses, and liabilities arising from:
- Customer's breach of these Terms and Conditions

## 11. ACCOUNT CLOSURE AND TERMINATION

### 11.1 Voluntary Closure

**Customer Initiated Closure:**
The Customer may request account closure by:
- Submitting a written request to any branch
- Filling the account closure form
- Surrendering all cards and cheque books
- Settling all outstanding obligations

**Closure Processing:**
- Account closure shall be processed within 7 working days of receipt of request
- Final account statement shall be provided
- Remaining balance shall be paid by draft or transfer to another account
- Applicable closure fees may apply

### 11.2 Bank Initiated Closure

**Grounds for Closure:**
The Bank may close Customer accounts for:
- Non-compliance with regulatory requirements
- Suspicious activities or fraud
- Dormant accounts exceeding regulatory thresholds
- Customer breach of Terms and Conditions
- Regulatory directives
- Customer request

**Closure Process:**
- The Bank shall provide written notice of closure (except in cases of fraud or regulatory requirements)
- Customer shall be given opportunity to provide explanation
- Final settlement shall be processed in accordance with regulatory requirements

### 11.3 Dormant Accounts

**Dormancy Definition:**
Accounts shall be considered dormant if no transactions occur for 6 months (savings) or 3 months (current).

**Dormant Account Management:**
- Dormant accounts shall be subject to dormancy fees
- Reactivation requires completion of reactivation process
- Unclaimed balances shall be transferred to the Unclaimed Funds Trust Fund in accordance with CBN regulations
